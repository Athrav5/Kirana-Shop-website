Aaradhaya Kirana & General Store

Family-owned since 1998 — fresh groceries, friendly service, and neighbourhood smiles.

About Our Shop

Aaradhaya Kirana began as a small pavement store and has grown into a trusted neighbourhood shop. We source fresh staples daily — grains, spices, produce and household essentials — with a focus on value, quality, and convenience.

Our Mission

To make everyday essentials accessible and affordable while giving personal service that big stores cannot match. We aim to be the corner-store you can rely on — for daily groceries, quick deliveries and friendly advice.

Core Values

Freshness & Quality

Daily procurement and careful selection so you get the best every time.

Trust

Transparent weights, honest pricing, and fair returns — always.

Community

We support local producers and are active in neighbourhood events.

What We Offer
